**Integrity constraints** are rules applied to database tables to ensure that the data stored is **accurate, consistent, valid, and reliable**.

In simple words:

> Integrity constraints **protect your database from wrong or meaningless data**.

They act like **guards** that check every data entry before it is stored in the database.
